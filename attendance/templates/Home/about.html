About Far Eastern Polytechnic College

Our Vision

FEPC envisions itself as an active agent and catalyst in the government's entire effort to make education more responsive to the evolving needs of students, aligned with local and international standards.

Our Mission

FEPC shall provide relevant educational programs and training leading to baccalaureate degrees. Establish strong academic linkages with industries, local government units, and other recognized professional organizations, and strengthen its commitment to corporate social responsibility. Strive to develop and maintain a pool of highly competent workforce to ensure the sustainability of the academic standards, goals and objectives.

Our Goals

1. To deliver quality and relevant higher education programs that equip students with the knowledge, skills and values necessary for local and global competitiveness.
2. To establish and sustain partnerships with industries, local government units (LGUs), and recognized organizations for collaborative development and experiential learning.
3. To build and sustain a competent academic and non-academic workforce that upholds high standards of education and professional service.
4. To align educational programs with the dynamic needs of the local and international community, ensuring relevance and adaptability in a global context.
5. To promote social accountability through meaningful outreach and extension programs that benefit the community and foster holistic development.

Our Objectives

1. Regularly review and upgrade academic programs in line with national and international standards.
2. Provide faculty and staff with continuous professional development opportunities to enhance competence and commitment.
3. Develop collaborative programs and initiatives with partner industries, LGUs, and organizations for student training and employment pathways.
4. Implement systems that monitor and evaluate academic and institutional performance for continuous improvement.
5. Integrate social responsibility into the curriculum and institutional activities, encouraging student and staff participation in civic and community initiatives.
6. Foster a learning environment that promotes critical thinking, innovation, an entrepreneurial mindset, and ethical leadership among students.
7. Ensure the efficient use of institutional resources to support academic and operational sustainability.
